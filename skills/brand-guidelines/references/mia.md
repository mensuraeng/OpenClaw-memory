# MIA — Brand Guidelines

Source: `/root/2nd-brain/04-projects/mia/brand-book-quiet-luxury.md`.

## Positioning

MIA is premium engineering performance: precision, silence, technical governance and high-standard delivery for discerning clients.

Core expression: **Precisão. Silêncio. Perfeição.**

## Visual direction

- Quiet luxury.
- Editorial, architectural, minimal.
- Large whitespace, strict hierarchy, fine lines.
- High contrast without visual noise.
- No mass-market construction tone.

## Official palette

- Pristine Stone: `#F9F9F7` — main background.
- Deep Obsidian: `#050505` — contrast background / hero / footer.
- Industrial Silver: `#A0A0A0` — brand accent.
- Primary text: `#1C1917`.
- Secondary text: `#737373`.

## Typography

- Headings/emphasis: **Playfair Display**, weight 400–600.
- Body/data/navigation: **Lato**, light weights for long text.
- Use italic sparingly for key terms: *MIA*, *Silêncio*, *End to End*, *Precisão*, *Execução*.

## Layout

- Hero can use dark background and centered logo/statement.
- Lines and borders: 1px, precise, restrained.
- Avoid heavy shadows, bright gradients, over-saturation.
- The page should feel calm, controlled and expensive without cliché luxury.

## Tone

- Confident but quiet.
- Premium but not ostentatious.
- Technical control as emotional relief.

## Avoid

- Gold clichés.
- “Obra dos sonhos” language.
- Urgency/scarcity tactics.
- Generic construction photos.
- Overexplaining.
