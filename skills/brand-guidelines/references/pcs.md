# PCS — Brand Guidelines

Source: `/root/2nd-brain/04-projects/pcs/identidade-visual.md`.

## Positioning

PCS is institutional engineering for public works, restoration, execution capacity, documentation, compliance and operational robustness.

## Visual direction

- Institutional, sober, robust, clear.
- Feels capable and documented, not flashy.
- Good for public works, technical reports, proposals, credentials and administrative material.

## Official palette

### Cinza escuro
- HEX: `#37444A`
- RGB: `55, 68, 74`
- CMYK: `75, 55, 50, 50`
- Pantone: `432 C`

### Warm red
- HEX: `#E74133`
- RGB: `231, 65, 51`
- CMYK: `0, 85, 80, 0`
- Pantone: `179 C`

### Off white
- HEX: `#F8F7F7`
- RGB: `248, 247, 247`
- CMYK: `3, 3, 3, 0`
- Pantone: `9043 C`

## Color roles

- Dark gray: institutional base, technical seriousness, structure.
- Warm red: accent, signature, emphasis, alerts.
- Off white: clean background and breathing space.

## Typography direction

- Sans-serif institutional.
- Strong headings, clear labels, low ornamentation.
- Tables and documental structure are appropriate.

## Tone

- Técnico-institucional.
- Clear, direct, formal when external.
- Emphasize capacity, governance, documentation, compliance and execution.

## Avoid

- MIA premium/luxury look.
- MENSURA PMO-only analytical look when the objective is institutional PCS.
- Too many colors.
- Aggressive marketing language.
- Unsupported legal/contractual claims.
