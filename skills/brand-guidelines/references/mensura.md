# MENSURA — Brand Guidelines

## Positioning

MENSURA is a technical-executive control tower for construction: schedule, CAPEX, risk, indicators, governance and decision support.

Core idea: **obra não atrasa por falta de esforço; atrasa por falta de sistema**.

## Visual direction

- Executive, technical, analytical, robust.
- Looks like a board-level risk report, not a construction brochure.
- High contrast, clean grids, dashboards, charts, lines, evidence blocks.
- Avoid generic engineering stock imagery.

## Palette

Official exact palette still needs canonical extraction/approval. Current safe inferred palette from existing presentation:

- Primary dark: `#000000` / near-black.
- Off-white background: `#F8F7F2` to `#FFFFFF`.
- Technical gray: `#A0A0A0` / `#C0C0C0`.
- Secondary dark gray: `#202020` / `#303030`.

Use as provisional until official brand tokens are extracted from source assets.

## Typography direction

- Headings: modern sans-serif, uppercase or tight executive hierarchy.
- Body: clean sans-serif with high readability.
- Data: tabular/monospace style acceptable for metrics.

## Tone

- Técnica, executiva, direta.
- Evidência antes de promessa.
- Trocar adjetivo por indicador.
- Trocar promessa por método.

## Good language

- “Torre de Controle”
- “governança executiva”
- “previsibilidade”
- “risco ativo”
- “CAPEX”
- “rituais de decisão”
- “métricas auditáveis”
- “antecipação de risco”

## Avoid

- “controle completo” sem evidência.
- “garantimos prazo”.
- Fiscalização genérica.
- Visual de obra residencial premium.
- Marketing vazio ou futurista demais.
