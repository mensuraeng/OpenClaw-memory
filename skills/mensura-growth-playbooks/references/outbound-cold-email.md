# Outbound / Cold Email

Use for MENSURA prospecting and follow-up.

## Preconditions

- List passed hygiene: dedupe, domain/DNS, suppression, bounce history, role-based filter, ICP score.
- Sender and signature confirmed.
- One offer only.
- No send without approval.

## Principles

- Write like a peer, not a vendor.
- Lead with the buyer's world, not our company.
- Personalization must connect to the problem.
- One clear low-friction ask.
- Short beats comprehensive.

## Preferred structure

1. Relevant observation or context.
2. Problem that usually follows.
3. MENSURA angle/proof.
4. Soft CTA.

Example CTA patterns:

- “Faz sentido eu te mandar um diagnóstico rápido do risco?”
- “Vale uma conversa de 20 min para ver se isso aparece nas suas obras?”
- “Se não for prioridade agora, me diga que eu encerro por aqui.”

## Avoid

- “Espero que esteja bem.”
- “Somos uma empresa líder.”
- Multiple links.
- Asking for 30 minutes in first touch unless the signal is strong.
- Follow-up “só passando para saber”.

## Follow-up cadence

- 3 to 5 touches max.
- Each follow-up adds a new angle: risk, benchmark, diagnostic, proof, break-up.
- Stop on unsubscribe, negative response, clear no-fit or sensitive reply.
