# Paid Ads — Planning and Controlled Execution

Use for Meta, LinkedIn and Google Ads planning, audits and campaign structure.

## Current Meta rule

- Budget cap: **R$ 0/dia**.
- Allowed: connect, read, audit, draft campaigns, audiences, UTMs, creatives, landing pages.
- Blocked: activate, publish with spend, budget increase, any charge.

## Campaign setup checklist

- Business/account correct.
- Payment method understood but no spend unless cap approved.
- Offer and CTA defined.
- Lead flow defined: Meta form, landing page, CRM, owner, response SLA.
- UTMs defined.
- Measurement defined: spend, CTR, CPL, qualified lead, meeting, proposal, opportunity.

## MENSURA first offer

- Diagnostic: “Diagnóstico Executivo de Previsibilidade de Obra”.
- Buyer: directors/owners/engineering/operations in builders/developers.
- Pain: lack of executive risk visibility, schedule slippage, CAPEX exposure.

## Optimization logic

- Low CTR: creative/angle/audience mismatch.
- High CPL: offer or landing/form issue.
- High lead volume, low quality: qualification questions or audience too broad.
- Spend without CRM response: stop, fix lead flow first.
