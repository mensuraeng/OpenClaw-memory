# Competitor Research

Use for competitors, alternatives, positioning gaps and battle cards.

## Research dimensions

- Positioning and headline.
- ICP and vertical focus.
- Offer and CTA.
- Services/capabilities.
- Proof/logos/cases.
- Pricing signals.
- Content/SEO strength.
- Weakness or gap, labeled as fact/inference.

## Output profile

- Snapshot date.
- Source URLs/files.
- What they claim.
- Who they target.
- Why buyers choose them.
- Where MENSURA/MIA/PCS can differentiate.
- Sales battle card: when we win, when we lose, objection response.

## Guardrail

Do not invent competitor weakness. Label inference clearly.
