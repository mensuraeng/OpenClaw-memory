# Conversion Copy / CRO

Use for landing pages, lead magnets, forms, page copy and CTA optimization.

## Page structure

1. Hero: buyer pain + clear outcome + CTA.
2. Problem: what is broken today.
3. Cost of inaction: delay, capital, rework, exposure, stress.
4. Method: how we diagnose/control/execute differently.
5. Proof: project, metric, artifact, case, process.
6. Offer: what the visitor gets.
7. Form/CTA: low friction, clear next step.

## CTA patterns

- “Solicitar diagnóstico executivo”
- “Avaliar risco da obra”
- “Agendar conversa técnica”
- “Receber checklist de previsibilidade”

## Form rules

- Ask only what affects qualification or routing.
- For MENSURA: role, company, number/value of obras, main pain, timeline.
- Too many fields reduce volume; too few fields reduce quality. Choose based on channel.

## Copy rule

Specific outcome beats abstract quality. Use “antecipar risco de prazo e CAPEX” over “soluções completas de engenharia”.
