# Customer Research / VOC

Use for extracting buyer language and signal from emails, calls, replies, proposals, reviews or public sources.

## Extract

- Jobs to be done: functional, emotional, social.
- Pain points: operational, financial, reputational.
- Trigger events: delay, claim, CAPEX risk, audit, executive pressure.
- Desired outcomes: buyer's own words.
- Objections: price, timing, trust, authority, internal capability.
- Alternatives: internal team, planner, fiscal, project manager, doing nothing.

## Confidence labels

- High: appears in 3+ independent sources, unprompted, consistent.
- Medium: appears in 2 sources or one segment.
- Low: single source or weak inference.

## Output

Return themes with exact quotes when possible, source path/date, confidence and recommended messaging use.
